Keyword Research Tools
Cómo elegir las palabras clave adecuadas ►

Keyword research is a fundamental part of SEO. Identifying the words and phrases that users search for in search engines can help you optimize your content and attract more traffic to your website. In this section, we will explore various tools that you can use to conduct effective keyword research, from the most basic to the most advanced.

Free Tools

Google Keyword Planner

Google Keyword Planner is a free tool provided by Google Ads. It is ideal for beginners and offers accurate data directly from Google.

  • Main features:

    • Keyword search.
    • Monthly search volume.
    • Competition level.
    • Related keyword ideas.

  • How to use it:

    1. Access Google Keyword Planner through your Google Ads account.
    2. Select "Discover new keywords."
    3. Enter a keyword or a URL related to your business.
    4. Review the suggestions and data provided.
Example:
If you enter "sports shoes," Google Keyword Planner might suggest keywords like "buy sports shoes," "cheap sports shoes," etc.

Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest is a free tool that offers a wide range of useful data for keyword research.

  • Main features:

    • Search volume.
    • SEO difficulty.
    • Cost per click (CPC).
    • Keyword suggestions.
    • Competitor analysis.

  • How to use it:

    1. Visit the Ubersuggest website.
    2. Enter a keyword or domain.
    3. Explore the suggestions and data provided.
Example:
If you enter "digital marketing," Ubersuggest might show you related keywords like "digital marketing strategies," "digital marketing courses," etc.

Paid Tools

SEMrush

SEMrush is an advanced SEO tool that offers a wide range of functionalities for keyword research.

  • Main features:

    • Keyword analysis.
    • Competitor research.
    • Position tracking.
    • Website audit.
    • Backlink analysis.

  • How to use it:

    1. Access SEMrush and create an account.
    2. Use the "Keyword Overview" tool to research keywords.
    3. Enter a keyword and review the data provided.
Example:
If you enter "SEO for beginners," SEMrush might show you the search volume, keyword difficulty, and the top pages ranking for that keyword.

Ahrefs

Ahrefs is another powerful tool for keyword research and competitor analysis.

  • Main features:

    • Keyword explorer.
    • Competitor analysis.
    • Website audit.
    • Backlink analysis.
    • Position tracking.

  • How to use it:

    1. Access Ahrefs and create an account.
    2. Use the "Keyword Explorer" to research keywords.
    3. Enter a keyword and review the data provided.
Example:
If you enter "SEO content," Ahrefs might show you the search volume, keyword difficulty, and the top pages ranking for that keyword.

Conclusion

Keyword research is essential for any SEO strategy. The tools we have discussed, both free and paid, offer a variety of functionalities that can help you identify the best keywords for your content. When choosing a tool, consider your specific needs and budget. Start with free tools if you are a beginner and, as your SEO strategy becomes more advanced, consider investing in paid tools for more detailed data and additional functionalities.

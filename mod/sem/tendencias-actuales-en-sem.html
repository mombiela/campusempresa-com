Introduction

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a crucial strategy for increasing a website's visibility in search results. With the advancement of technology and changes in consumer behavior, SEM trends are constantly evolving. This topic will cover current trends in SEM, from basic concepts to advanced techniques.

1. Automation and Machine Learning in SEM

Automation and machine learning are transforming SEM, allowing advertisers to optimize their campaigns more efficiently.

  • Bid Automation: Platforms like Google Ads use algorithms to automatically adjust bids in real-time.
  • Dynamic Ads: Automatic generation of ads based on website content.
  • Smart Segmentation: Use of machine learning to identify and segment audiences more precisely.

Example code for bid automation in Google Ads:

from google.ads.google_ads.client import GoogleAdsClient
from google.ads.google_ads.errors import GoogleAdsException

def set_automated_bidding(client, customer_id, campaign_id):
    campaign_service = client.get_service("CampaignService", version="v6")
    campaign_operation = client.get_type("CampaignOperation", version="v6")
    campaign = campaign_operation.update
    campaign.resource_name = campaign_service.campaign_path(customer_id, campaign_id)
    campaign.bidding_strategy_type = client.get_type("BiddingStrategyTypeEnum", version="v6").TARGET_CPA
    client.copy_from(campaign_operation.update_mask, protobuf_helpers.field_mask(None, campaign._pb))
    try:
        campaign_service.mutate_campaigns(customer_id, [campaign_operation])
        print(f"Automated bidding set for campaign {campaign_id}")
    except GoogleAdsException as ex:
        print(f"An error occurred: {ex}")

# Replace with appropriate values
client = GoogleAdsClient.load_from_storage()
set_automated_bidding(client, 'INSERT_CUSTOMER_ID_HERE', 'INSERT_CAMPAIGN_ID_HERE')

2. Video Advertising and YouTube

Video content is booming and YouTube has become an essential platform for SEM.

  • TrueView Ads: You pay only when users watch the entire ad or interact with it.
  • Bumper Ads: Short 6-second ads that cannot be skipped.
  • Outstream Ads: Video ads that appear outside of YouTube, on partner websites and apps.

Comparison of ad types on YouTube:

| Ad Type | Duration | Cost | Location | |-----------------|----------|------|-----------| | TrueView | 15-30 sec| CPV | YouTube | | Bumper | 6 sec | CPM | YouTube | | Outstream | Variable | CPM | Web/App |

3. Voice Search and Virtual Assistants

With the rise in use of devices like Google Home and Amazon Echo, voice search is changing how users interact with search engines.

  • Optimization for Voice Search: Use of natural language and frequently asked questions.
  • Featured Snippets: Appearing in Google's position zero.
  • Long-tail Keywords: Longer, more specific phrases that users tend to use in voice searches.

4. Social Media Advertising and SEM

Integrating SEM with social media advertising can enhance campaign results.

  • Retargeting: Showing ads to users who have already interacted with your website.
  • Lookalike Audiences: Finding new customers similar to your existing ones.
  • Stories Ads: Using ad formats in Instagram and Facebook stories.

Conclusion

Current trends in SEM are marked by automation, video content, voice search, and integration with social media. Staying updated with these trends and effectively applying them can significantly improve the performance of your search engine marketing campaigns.
